Privacy Policy

Fly the World: Bird Flap

Last updated: August 21, 2025

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how the mobile game Fly the World: Bird Flap ("the Game", "we", "us", or "our") handles user information. This game is developed and published by an independent developer and is available for free on Google Play.

We are committed to respecting your privacy. This policy explains what data may be collected, how it's used, and your choices.

2. Data Collection

We do not directly collect or store any personal data from users of the Game.

However, the Game uses third-party services that may collect information automatically to enable advertising or usage measurement. These services operate under their own privacy policies.

3. Third-Party Services

The Game uses Google AdMob to serve advertisements, including banner ads and optional rewarded ads.

AdMob may collect and process data such as:

  • Device information (e.g., model, OS version)
  • IP address
  • Approximate location (for ad targeting)
  • Usage statistics (e.g., ad views, interactions)

This data is collected automatically by AdMob, not by the developer of the Game.

More on AdMob’s data practices: https://policies.google.com/technologies/ads

4. Children's Privacy

The Game is intended for users aged 13 and older. It is not specifically directed toward children under 13 and does not contain any child-targeted content.

We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child under 13 has provided personal data through the Game, please contact us so we can delete it.

5. User Accounts and Input

  • No login or account creation is required.
  • No text input or nickname collection is used.
  • Game progress is stored locally on the user’s device.

6. Storage and Security

No personal data is stored by the developer on external servers.

The Game does not upload gameplay data or device information to the developer.

Any data collected by third-party services is handled and secured by those services.

7. Ads and Personalization

The Game displays banner ads and rewarded ads via Google AdMob.

Ad content is controlled by Google’s ad network. Some ads may be personalized based on user behavior or location (as determined by AdMob).

Users can manage ad personalization in their Google Account settings:
https://adssettings.google.com

8. User Rights

Since no personal data is collected directly, the developer does not store any information that can be accessed or deleted.

Questions about AdMob’s data practices should be directed to Google.

11. Consent
